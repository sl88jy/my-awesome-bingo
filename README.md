## âœ¨ What is Soc Ops?

**Soc Ops** (Social Operations) is a digital bingo game designed to make in-person gatherings more engaging. Instead of marking random numbers, players mingle and find people who match fun prompts like "has lived in another country" or "speaks more than 2 languages."

**The Goal:** Get 5 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) by finding people who match your bingo squares!
